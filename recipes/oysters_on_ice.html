Oysters served with lemons, laying on ice

Rather preparing, than cooking

Oysters are enjoyable as snacks or as a starting dish.

With their salty taste and the lemon infused sea-water which they get served with, they are very refreshing and delicate.

Things you will need

  • Oyster Knife
  • Oysters
  • Lemon(s)
  • Crushed ice
  • big plate

Step by step

  1. Open the Oysters with the Oyster knife, and don't spill the sea water

  2. Split the lemons into quarters

  3. Squeeze some lemon juice on to the opened Oysters

  4. Put the crushed ice on the plate

  5. Place the Oysters and some of the lemon quarters on the ice

  6. Serve
